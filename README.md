#  Sales and Profit Performance Dashboard (Excel)

##  Problem Statement

Businesses dealing with multi-year transactional data often struggle to quickly identify which products, customers, and time periods drive the most value. This project transforms raw sales data into an **interactive Excel dashboard** to support faster, data-driven business decisions.

---

##  Objective

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance across 4 years (2021–2024)
- Identify top and low performing product categories and sub-categories
- Understand monthly sales trends and seasonality
- Compare profit contribution by category across years
- Enable interactive analysis using slicers

---

##  Dataset

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Time Period | 2021 – 2024 |
| Categories | Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology |
| Key Fields | Order Date, Category, Sub-Category, Customer, State, Sales, Profit |

---

##  Tools & Techniques

- **Microsoft Excel** — Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Map Chart, Dashboard Design & Formatting

---

##  Dashboard Features

- KPI cards for **Total Sales** and **Total Profit**
- Year-wise profit breakdown by category
- Sub-category sales comparison (horizontal bar chart)
- Top 5 customers by profit contribution
- Monthly sales trend for seasonality analysis
- State-wise sales map (geographic distribution)
- Customer count by year
- Interactive slicers for **Category**, **Year**, and **Month**

---

##  Key Insights

- **Total Sales: $19,28,888 | Total Profit: $2,47,961** across all four years
- **Phones and Chairs** are the highest revenue-generating sub-categories
- **Technology profit peaked in 2023 at $39,774** — nearly double its 2021 figure of $22,593
- **Furniture consistently underperforms**, with profit as low as $5,477 in 2021
- **Top customer Tamara Chand generated $8,981 in profit** — top 5 customers contribute a disproportionate share
- **Sales dip sharply in February** and recover steadily, peaking in **November–December**
- **California dominates state-wise sales at $3,90,146**, significantly ahead of other states
- Customer count peaked in **2023 at 2,586** before declining to **1,634 in 2024** — a trend worth investigating

## How to Use

1. Download `Sales_and_Profit_Dashboard.xlsx`
2. Open in Microsoft Excel (2016 or later recommended)
3. Use the slicers on the left panel to filter by **Category**, **Year**, or **Month**
4. All charts and KPIs update dynamically based on your selection
